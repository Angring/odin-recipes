Indian Chicken Curry

Enjoy the lavishly delicious taste of chickencurry at home with this easy-to-follow recipe

Ingredients

  • Garam masala
  • Coriander
  • Turmeric
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Tomato
  • Yogurt
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Chicken

Steps

  1. Partially cook the seasoned chicken breasts in a large pan, removing them after they're browned.
  2. Cook garlic, onions, and ginger in the same pan to create the flavor base.
  3. Once the onion are translucent, add in spices, tomatoes, yogurt, and chicken to cook thoroughly.
  4. Cover and simmer for about 20 minutes.
  5. Finish with fresh herbs and a squeeze of lemon.
