Chai Tea Latte

Simple and delicious either hot or iced

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup boiling water
  • 1 chai tea bag
  • 1 ½ teaspoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • ¾ cup milk

Steps

  1. Pour boiling water over chai tea bag in a mug; let sleep 4 to 6 minutes. Remove and discard tea bag.
  2. Stir honey and sugar into tea to dissolve. Stir milk through tea to serve.
